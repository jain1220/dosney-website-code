Download DisneyPlus

DisneyPlus is the ultimate streaming destination for entertainment from Disney, Pixar, Marvel, Star Wars, and National Geographic.




How to Download DisneyPlus...


  1. Open the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android) on your device.
  2. Search for "Disney+" in the search bar.
  3. Locate the official Disney+ app in the search results.
  4. Tap on the app, then tap the "Download" or "Install" button.
  5. Once the app is downloaded and installed, open it.
  6. Log in with your Disney+ account or sign up if you don't have an account yet.


Download for Web Browsers :


  • Visit the Disney+ website.
  • If necessary, sign up for a Disney+ account.
  • Once signed in, you can stream content directly from the website.


Please note that the availability of the Disney+ app on certain devices may vary by region.
Additionally, the steps may have changed, so it's always a good idea to check the official Disney+ website or the app store on your device for the most up-to-date instructions.

Installation For Smart TVs:
  1. On your TV Connected Device.
  2. Navigate to your TV's app store or platform (e.g., Samsung Smart Hub, LG Content Store).
  3. Search for "Disney+" using the search feature
  4. Locate the Disney+ app in the results and select it
  5. Open the Disney+ app and sign up
