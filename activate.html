How to Activate DisneyPlus

  • Open a web browser on your computer.
  • Type https://www.disneyplus.com/ into the address bar and press Enter.
  • Click on "Log In" and enter the email address and password associated with your Disney+ account
  • Once logged in, you can explore the Disney+ library. Browse through the various categories, including Disney, Pixar, Marvel, Star Wars, and National Geographic.
  • Choose a movie or TV show you want to watch and click on it to start streaming.
